Welcome to KEMRI Help and Support

If you're facing any issues, have questions, or need assistance with our research services or systems, you've come to the right place. Below, we've provided answers to frequently asked questions (FAQ), troubleshooting tips, and contact details for further support from KEMRI.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
1. How do I access KEMRI research reports?

To access KEMRI research reports, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the "Research Reports" section on the KEMRI website.
  2. Click on the "Download Reports" button.
  3. Select the report category you're interested in and click on the report title.
  4. Download the PDF of the report by clicking the "Download" button next to the report.

2. How can I submit a research proposal to KEMRI?

To submit a research proposal, please:

  1. Visit the "Research Proposals" section on our platform.
  2. Click the "Submit Proposal" button.
  3. Fill in the required details, including the research title, objectives, and funding sources.
  4. Submit the proposal by clicking the "Submit" button. You will receive an acknowledgment email after submission.

3. What should I do if I can't find a research report?

If you are unable to locate a specific research report:

  • Ensure you have selected the correct category for the report.
  • Try using keywords related to your topic in the search bar.
  • If you still cannot find the report, please contact our support team at support@kemri.org.

4. What if I encounter issues with downloading reports?

If you're unable to download reports:

  • Check your internet connection to ensure it's stable.
  • Try using a different browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • If the issue persists, contact our technical support team for assistance.

5. Can I collaborate with KEMRI on a research project?

Yes, KEMRI collaborates with international and local institutions on various research projects. To start a collaboration, please visit our "Collaboration" page and fill out the collaboration form. Our research collaboration team will reach out to you for further discussions.

6. How do I get in touch with KEMRI's technical support team?

If you're experiencing technical difficulties with our website or services:

  • Ensure your internet connection is stable.
  • Update your browser to the latest version.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  • If the issue persists, reach out to our technical support team at support@kemri.org.

7. What if I need more information on a specific research project?

If you need detailed information about a research project, you can find project details in the "Research Projects" section. If you need further clarification, you can contact the project lead directly through the contact details provided on the project page.

System Requirements

To ensure that you can access and use our services smoothly, please make sure your system meets the following requirements:

  • Operating System: Windows 7 or later, macOS 10.10 or later, Linux
  • Browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari (latest versions)
  • PDF Reader: Any PDF reader (Adobe Acrobat Reader, Foxit Reader, etc.)
  • Internet Speed: A stable internet connection (recommended speed of 5 Mbps or higher)
System Limitations

While we strive to provide a seamless experience, there are a few limitations to keep in mind:

  • Only one PDF per research report can be uploaded.
  • The system is optimized for use with modern browsers. Older browsers may not support all features.
  • PDF files must be under 10 MB in size. If your file exceeds this limit, try compressing it before uploading.
Technical Support

If you encounter any technical difficulties while using our platform, please follow these steps:

  • Ensure that your internet connection is stable.
  • Update your browser to the latest version.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies to improve performance.
  • If the issue persists, contact our technical support team for further assistance at support@kemri.org.
